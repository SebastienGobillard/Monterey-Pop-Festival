Presentation

For the Monterey Pop Festival’s 50th anniversary, we wanted to make you relive the big moments of this festival that we will called later the « summer of love ». Monterey is a little inshore city 2 hours in the south of San Francisco. It is known also for the Monterey Jazz Festival and for the Monterey Folk Festival.

A lot of artists were influenced by the 1967 Monterey pop festival’s artists and a lot of cover be tributes were payed. Some of theme accepted to pay homage to those artist participating in this event.

Bands

For this anniversary we're having the pleasure to welcome a band which has already been in the 1967 festival : quicksilver messenger service.

This event aims to gather a public as large as it was in 1967.
